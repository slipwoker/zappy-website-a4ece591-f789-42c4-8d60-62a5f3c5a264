About Aviv

Creative Vision.
Strategic Depth.

I'm Aviv Msika — a multidisciplinary creative professional working at the intersection of strategy and visual artistry. With a background spanning social media management, creative direction, professional photography, and video editing, I offer a seamless, end-to-end service built for brands that refuse to compromise on quality.

My approach to social media management goes beyond scheduling posts. I develop cohesive brand narratives, design scroll-stopping visuals, and produce cinematic content — all in-house, all under one creative roof. From strategy to production, every element is crafted with intention.

What I Do

  • Social Media Strategy Platform-native content planning, audience growth, and engagement-driven campaigns tailored for lifestyle and e-commerce brands.
  • Creative Direction Art-directed shoots, moodboarding, and brand visual identity that translate seamlessly across digital touchpoints.
  • Photography & Videography Professional stills and motion capture for culinary, lifestyle, and product contexts — bright, editorial, and on-brand.
  • Video Editing Reels, TikToks, and long-form edits crafted with rhythm, pacing, and a keen eye for storytelling that converts.

From Strategy
to Polished Delivery

Every project follows a deliberate, end-to-end methodology — blending strategic clarity with meticulous creative execution. This is how an idea becomes a campaign that performs across social channels and beyond.

01

Discovery & Consultation

We begin with a deep-dive conversation to understand your brand identity, audience, objectives, and competitive landscape. This intake shapes the entire creative direction — ensuring every decision that follows is grounded in real strategy, not assumption.

02

Concept & Planning

With a clear strategic foundation, we develop a tailored content plan — mood boards, visual references, messaging pillars, and a production schedule. This phase transforms your brand goals into a cohesive creative blueprint ready for execution.

03

Production & Editing

From on-location photography and video capture to meticulous post-production — every image is retouched, every reel is cut with intention. This is where the creative vision comes to life with precision, warmth, and a distinctly elevated aesthetic.

04

Delivery & Launch

Polished assets are packaged and delivered in formats optimised for each social channel and campaign. Final files are accompanied by a scheduling strategy and caption guidance — so your content arrives ready to publish and built to perform.
